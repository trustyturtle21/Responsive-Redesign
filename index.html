Who Are We?

The Rhode Island Kung Fu Club is a not-for-profit organization based in Providence and Pawtucket, RI and has taught the martial and cultural arts of Kung Fu and Chinese Lion Dance to Providence-area youth since 2007. The RIKFC shares these arts with both Chinese and non-Chinese audiences throughout the state in many different settings, from lion performances to martial arts competitions. Venues have included WaterFire in Providence, Pawtucket Arts Festival, FirstWorks Providence, Rhode Island Heritage Festival, and numerous other cultural events and fundraisers for charities and schools. The local Chinese community also calls on the RIKFC to perform at cultural events throughout the year, particularly at Lunar New Year.

RIKFC members have participated in numerous martial arts competitions throughout New England and the east coast. Our mission is to utilize martial arts training to assist young people in their maturation and growth. The effort and commitment required to develop Kung Fu and lion dance skills cultivates patience and perseverance, which are cornerstone ideals of the Rhode Island Kung Fu Club. We believe developing these traits will serve our members well in the future.
